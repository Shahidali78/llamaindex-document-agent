MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on March 3, 2025
("Effective Date") by and between Northwind Analytics LLC ("Provider"), a
Delaware limited liability company, and Acme Retail Corporation ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Provider shall deliver cloud-based data analytics services, including monthly
reporting dashboards and quarterly business reviews, as described in Exhibit A.

2. TERM
The initial term is twelve (12) months commencing on the Effective Date and
will automatically renew for successive twelve-month periods unless either party
provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days before the end
of the then-current term.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT
Client shall pay Provider a monthly subscription fee of $8,500 USD. An initial
onboarding fee of $15,000 USD is due within fifteen (15) days of the Effective
Date. Invoices are payable net thirty (30) days. Late payments accrue interest
at 1.5% per month.

4. OBLIGATIONS
Provider shall maintain 99.9% monthly uptime and respond to critical support
tickets within four (4) hours. Client shall provide timely access to required
data sources and designate a primary point of contact, Jane Whitfield.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party agrees to protect the other party's Confidential Information and to
use it solely for purposes of performing under this Agreement.

6. LIABILITY
Provider's total aggregate liability shall not exceed the fees paid by Client in
the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Neither party is liable for
indirect or consequential damages.

7. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach remains uncured
thirty (30) days after written notice. Client may terminate for convenience with
ninety (90) days' written notice, subject to payment for services rendered.

8. SIGNATURES
Provider: Robert Chen, CEO, Northwind Analytics LLC
Client: Maria Gonzalez, VP Operations, Acme Retail Corporation
Date signed: March 3, 2025
