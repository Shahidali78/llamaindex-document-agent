SOFTWARE SUBSCRIPTION & SUPPORT AGREEMENT

This Software Subscription & Support Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective
June 12, 2025 between Brightpath Software Inc. ("Vendor") and Acme Retail
Corporation ("Customer").

1. LICENSE & SERVICES
Vendor grants Customer a non-exclusive subscription to the Brightpath Inventory
Platform, including standard support and product updates.

2. TERM
The subscription runs for twenty-four (24) months from the effective date and
renews automatically for twelve-month terms unless cancelled with thirty (30)
days' written notice prior to renewal.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT
The annual subscription fee is $96,000 USD, billed annually in advance. A
one-time implementation fee of $20,000 USD applies. Invoices are due net forty-
five (45) days. Late balances accrue interest at 1.0% per month.

4. SERVICE LEVELS
Vendor commits to 99.5% monthly availability and will respond to high-priority
incidents within eight (8) hours during business hours.

5. DATA & SECURITY
Vendor will encrypt Customer data at rest and in transit and undergo an annual
SOC 2 Type II audit. Customer's administrator of record is Daniel Brooks.

6. LIABILITY
Vendor's aggregate liability is capped at the total fees paid in the prior
twelve (12) months. Consequential damages are excluded for both parties.

7. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate for uncured material breach after a thirty (30) day
cure period. Fees already paid are non-refundable except where required by law.

8. SIGNATURES

Customer: Maria Gonzalez, VP Operations, Acme Retail Corporation
